# Privacy Policy — Jugnu

Last updated: 17 January 2026

Jugnu (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the Jugnu mobile application (the “App”).  
This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our App.

By using Jugnu, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

## App Purpose

Jugnu is a communication app that enables users to connect through one-to-one voice and video calls.

## Permissions We Use and Why

### Camera Permission
Jugnu may request access to your device’s camera.

- Used only for video calling features
- Access occurs only during an active call
- Camera is never accessed in the background
- We do not record, store, or share camera content

### Microphone Permission
Jugnu may request access to your device’s microphone.

- Used only for voice and video calls
- Audio is active only during a call
- We do not record, store, or monitor call audio

### Foreground Service (Camera & Microphone)
Jugnu uses foreground services to ensure calls continue reliably when the app is running in the background.

- Required for ongoing voice and video calls
- A visible notification is shown during active calls

### Internet & Network State
Jugnu requires internet access to enable real-time communication and notifications.

### Notifications
Jugnu may send notifications related to incoming calls or app activity.
You can manage notification permissions in your device settings.

### Wake Lock & Audio Settings
These permissions help maintain call quality by preventing interruptions during active calls.

## Data Collection

Jugnu does **not** collect or store:
- Call audio
- Call video
- Personal photos or videos
- Contacts
- Messages

Any data processed during calls is used only to provide real-time communication functionality.

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

## Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data transmitted through the App.
